Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This is a very famous italian dish called Spaghetti Carbonara.

The dish itself is very simple but has a long history and is not

that easy to make, but give you a start and try it out!

Ingredients (4P)

  • 500gr Spaghetti
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 1 egg
  • 100gr parmesan cheese
  • 250gr guanciale
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot and add the spaghetti when the water is boiling.

    Do not forget to salt the water!

  2. While the spaghetti are cooking you can make the sauce. First grain

    the parmesan cheese and add it to a bowl. Add the 4 egg yolks, the

    whole egg, few tablespoons of the pasta water and mix well.

  3. Few minutes before the pasta is cooked "al dente" (read on the packege

    how long to cook the pasta), take another pan and cook the guanciale

    without any oil until golden brown, then turn of the heat.

  4. Now add the pasta to the guanciale and mix. Add also the carbonara

    sauce but be careful that the pan isn't to hot anymore.

  5. Finally you can serve your pasta and garnish with pepper an parmesan. Bon appetit!
